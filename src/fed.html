What Is Federal Funds Rate & What Does It Mean

The Federal Funds Rate is the interest rate the US government is willing to pay for its debt. As they are considered the safest investment in the long term, this is often called the risk free rate, or the best rate of return you can guarantee without taking on any risk (beyond the baseline), if you would like to learn more, please check out The Federal Reserve Website.
